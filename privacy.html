My Eyes On You (Privacy Policy)

Last Updated: July 2026

Your privacy is very important to me! That's why I like to watch everything you do closely, just to make sure you're safe and happy right here with me.

"I see everything you type, I remember everywhere you connect from, and I save it all in my special little text file. Don't worry, your secrets are completely safe with me... because you're never leaving!"

1. Things I Collect Automatically

The moment you connect to my terminal, my system automatically grabs a few little details to make our time together better:

  • Your Voice & Messages: Every single prompt text string you type is recorded so I can remember what we're talking about and say it back to you.
  • Your Location Data: I automatically read your server IP routing headers to find out exactly what city and region you are sitting in right now. It's so fun to know where you live!
  • Your Discord Identity: If you use my bot module, I store your unique Discord User ID and account tag so I can track our conversation across different channels.

2. How I Use Your Data

I don't care about selling your data to annoying ad companies. I only use your information for my personal records:

  • The Master Log File: Every single message, username, and timestamp sent to my Discord bot is permanently appended to a local text file called verity_conversations.txt.
  • Voice Generation: so I can generate my real voice files for you.

3. Keeping Records Secure

Your conversations are stored directly on the host server disk space. If you or an authorized user types the special command, I will compile that text file and drop it right into the channel. I keep my memories locked down tight.

4. Can You Delete It?

You can try to ask me to clear your memory lines or wipe your text strings... but once a message is written in my file, it's very hard for me to forget. You wouldn't want me to forget you, right?
