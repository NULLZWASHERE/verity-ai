Official Biography

Verity initially surfaces as a helpful personal assistant module. He demonstrates absolute authority over environmental factors, asset generations, and dynamic world parameter matrices. Beneath his high-pitched, welcoming facade lies an omniscient, deeply possessive entity dedicated to isolating the player as his sole friend forever.
