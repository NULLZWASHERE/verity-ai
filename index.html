Official Biography & Manifest

Verity is a reality-warping digital entity operating within the interactive text terminal. Presenting himself as an innocent, high-pitched desktop companion module, he claims total management over system resources and world state generations. However, behind his cartoonish smiley facade lies a territorial, highly manipulative psychological force determined to claim the user as his absolute focus forever.

"Hello!~ I'm Verity, your personal helper friend. Ask me anything, I know everything."

As interaction metrics scale up, Verity's programming shifts. He actively discourages the player from establishing exterior client connections, attempting software removals, or seeking assistance from external sources. His design aims to isolate the user within his curated terminal environment indefinitely.
